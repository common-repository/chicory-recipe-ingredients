Chicory makes recipe monetization easy. Grow revenue for your blog and offer site visitors the ability to shop directly from your recipe content.
== Description ==

Monetize your recipe card with contextual, in-recipe ads from food advertisers that are relevant to your site. Offer your audience a seamless shopping experience at 70+ integrated retailers, including Instacart, Walmart, and Kroger. Join Chicory’s network of 5,200+ major food publishers like Food Network, The Pioneer Woman, and Delish in trusting our solution.  Available in the U.S. only.

Our [Premium](https://chicory.co/chicory-for-food-bloggers) advertisements are placed within the recipe card and targeted to either ingredient keywords or recipe categories. [Recipe Activation](https://chicory.co/chicory-for-food-bloggers) technology makes it easy for site visitors to cart the full recipe at their preferred retailer.

Get started by installing the plugin and completing [this form](https://portal.chicory.co/become-a-chicory-recipe-partner) to set up payment and begin earning revenue. By signing up, you will gain access to Chicory’s self-service insights and reporting dashboard, Partner Portal, which will allow you to understand which recipes and ingredients are driving the most revenue.

== Installation ==

1. Download and install Chicory Shoppable Recipes from WordPress
2. Complete [this sign-up form](https://portal.chicory.co/become-a-chicory-recipe-partner) to set up payment and begin earning revenue

Once installation is complete and you have filled out the above form, you will have access to Chicory’s Partner Portal, a self-service insights and reporting platform. You will be able to access recipe data and track ad revenue to help answer questions like *Which one of my recipes is the most profitable?* and *Which ingredients drive the most revenue for my site?*
